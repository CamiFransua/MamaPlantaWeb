About us

We're a dynamic duo blending Italian and Argentinian flavors. Guided by our passion for delicious food and sustainable living, we embarked on an adventure to create a vegan pasta bar that reflects our diverse cultural backgrounds and commitment to quality ingredients. At Mama PLANTA, every dish is a celebration of our heritage.

Come and experience

We've created a cozy haven in the heart of Cologne to invite you to experience the warmth and flavors of Italy and Argentina.
